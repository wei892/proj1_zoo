Horse

Horses have four powerful legs, two eyes, a nose, ears and a tail. Wild horse populations are found around the world, from the Mongolian steppes to Africa, Europe, North America and beyond. Unlike other ungulates, they don't have horns. They can run fast for long stretches and are powerful animals. Humans have long domesticated horses as beasts of burden because of their enormous strength.
